Before you begin:

1. Learn clicking methods: normal, butterfly, jitter, dragclick
a) Normal clicking: clicking normally with one finger
b) Butterfly clicking: clicking on your mouse with two fingers, in quick sequence
c) Jitter clicking: holding your hand in place and tensing it up so it shivers. Dangerous in the longrun, and not recommended.
d) Dragclick: dragging your finger over your mouse in such a way that it creates friction and causes a lot of clicks in a row. Wears out the mouse, and requires one with a low cooldown and bad switches
2. Clicking methods are easier to do if your mouse is capable of doubleclicking. Clicking on your mouse a certain way should cause it to click two or three times in a row instead of one. This is extremely common with cheaper switches
3. CPS (clicks-per-second) are important, but so is precision. You can be good at bridging with 1cps, and bad at it with 20cps. A higher cps isnt necessary as Hypixeč doesnt register CPS above a certain number, and even then precision and practice is much more important. 10-12 CPS is more than enough
4. You can practice by making your own creative world (unlimited blocks), or in certain public Minecraft servers
