Godbridge

Basics

Godbridge is one of the most difficult commonly used methods and it is the most difficult one.
It is not impossible or the most difficult method in existence

Direct bridge instructions

1. Place down a block and get on top of it.
2. Crouch and go backwards until you hit the edge of the block.
3. Still holding crouch, look down and position yourself at this exact spot.
4. Once you've placed a new block, let go of crouch and hold the S and D or A and S buttons.
5. You can either time your clicks or keep clicking. Use butterfly or dragclick.

Tip! Make sure you find an angle at which you can keep walking without falling off. Recenter yourself if you need to

Direct bridge instructions

1. Place down a block and get on top of it.
2. Crouch and go backwards until you hit the edge of the block.
3. Still holding crouch, look down and position yourself diagonally
4. Once you've placed two new blocks, hold S and D or A and S, depeneding on your preference
5. You can either time your clicks or keep clicking. Use butterfly or dragclick.

More tips:

1. Its good to practice bridging both ways. While you turn around to position yourself, someone can always knock you off
2. Practice multiple bridging methods
3. As useful as breezily, witchly, godbridge etc are, it is always important to also be good at ninjabridging
4. Combine bridging methods and find what youre best at
