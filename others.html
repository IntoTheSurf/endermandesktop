Other Speedbridging Methods

These methods are mostly used by sweaters and are very difficult to master.

Tellybridge

Jump off the ledge, turn around 180 degrees and place blocks while dragging your mouse, then turn around 180, land, jump and turn 180 degrees, place blocks, repeat

Moonwalk

Hold an angle between 45 and 90 degrees. Hold the "S" key and spam the "D" key. You might have to hold it to get back in place.

Derpbridge

You have to continuously move your camera back and forth while pressing down S and continuously clicking your mouse. This in turn makes you strafe

Tips:

1. Do not attempt these until you've mastered clicking and at the very least godbridging
2. These methods are extremely difficult and take a LOT of rigorous practice
3. They're the fastest, but the payback generally isnt worth it.
4. For more information, look them up on YouTube. Some Youtubers that specialize in difficult bridges are Bedless Noob, Teddynator, itslizard
